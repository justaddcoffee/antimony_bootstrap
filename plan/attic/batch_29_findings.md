## 6. Specific Extractable Parameters for ODE Modeling

### Tau Turnover (PMC4621881)
- **In vivo tau half-life (mouse model):**
  - Intracellular soluble tau: t1/2 = ~11 days
  - Extracellular tau: t1/2 = ~17 days (estimated from text)
  - Cell culture tau half-life range: 5-60 hours
- **Extractable as:** First-order degradation: `k_deg_tau = ln(2) / t1/2`
- **Rate type:** MA (mass action, first-order)
- **Antimony:** `J_tau_deg: Tau_native -> ; k_deg_tau * Tau_native * Neuron`

### Tau Acetylation (PMC4598295)
- **Mechanism:** p300 acetyltransferase acetylates tau at K174; SIRT1 deacetylates
- **Key finding:** K174Q (acetyl-mimicking) slows tau turnover
- **Salsalate inhibits p300, enhances tau turnover, reduces tau levels
- **Extractable as:**
  - Tau acetylation: `J_tau_acet: Tau_native -> Tau_acetylated; k_acet * p300 * Tau_native`
  - Tau deacetylation: `J_tau_deacet: Tau_acetylated -> Tau_native; k_deacet * SIRT1 * Tau_acetylated`
- **Rate type:** MA (mass action, second-order)

### Abeta Aggregation Kinetics (PMC4595974)
- **Mechanism:** Brichos chaperone inhibits secondary nucleation on fibril surfaces
- **Key reactions:**
  - Primary nucleation: `J_prim_nuc: n*AB42_monomer -> AB42_oligomer; k_n * AB42_monomer^n`
  - Secondary nucleation (fibril-catalyzed): `J_sec_nuc: m*AB42_monomer + AB42_fibril -> AB42_oligomer + AB42_fibril; k_2 * AB42_monomer^m * AB42_fibril`
  - Elongation: `J_elong: AB42_monomer + AB42_fibril -> AB42_fibril; k_plus * AB42_monomer * AB42_fibril`
- **Rate type:** custom_conc_per_time (nucleation-dependent polymerization)
- **Note:** Specific rate constants require fitting from ThT kinetic curves in paper

### Gamma-Secretase Activity (PMC4647268)
- **Mechanism:** FAD mutations alter gamma-secretase cleavage specificity
- **Key finding:** Production rates of Abeta38, Abeta40, Abeta42 measured from brain DRMs
- **Substrate:** C99 (CTFbeta) at 5 uM saturating concentration
- **Extractable as:**
  - `J_gamma_40: CTFbeta -> AB40 + AICD; Vmax_40 * CTFbeta / (Km_40 + CTFbeta)`
  - `J_gamma_42: CTFbeta -> AB42 + AICD; Vmax_42 * CTFbeta / (Km_42 + CTFbeta)`
- **Rate type:** custom (Michaelis-Menten)

### Abeta Clearance Pathways (PMC4694579)
- **Key quantitative insight:** ~75% of extracellular Abeta cleared via BBB, ~10% via ISF bulk flow
- **Clearance routes:**
  - BBB transcytosis (LRP1-mediated): 75% of eAbeta clearance
  - Glymphatic/ISF bulk flow (AQP4-dependent): significant fraction
  - Enzymatic degradation (neprilysin, IDE)
  - Microglial phagocytosis
  - Meningeal lymphatic drainage
- **Extractable as partitioned clearance:**
  - `J_AB_BBB: AB42_BrainISF -> AB42_Plasma; k_BBB * AB42_BrainISF * LRP1_BBB`
  - `J_AB_ISF: AB42_BrainISF -> AB42_CSF; k_ISF_bulk * AB42_BrainISF`
  - `J_AB_enzymatic: AB42_BrainISF -> ; k_NEP * Neprilysin * AB42_BrainISF + k_IDE * IDE * AB42_BrainISF`
  - `J_AB_microglia: AB42_BrainISF -> ; k_phago * Microglia_activated * AB42_BrainISF`
- **Rate type:** MA and custom (Michaelis-Menten for enzymatic)

### ApoE-Abeta Interactions (PMC4641345)
- **Mechanism:** ApoE4 slows Abeta clearance, leads to higher Abeta concentrations
- **Key finding:** Murine vs human ApoE4 differentially facilitate amyloid pathology
- **Extractable as:**
  - `J_ApoE_AB_bind: ApoE + AB42 -> ApoE_AB42_complex; kon_ApoE_AB * ApoE * AB42`
  - `J_ApoE_AB_clear: ApoE_AB42_complex -> ; k_clear_ApoE_AB * ApoE_AB42_complex * LRP1`
- **Rate type:** MA (binding), custom (receptor-mediated clearance)

### Abeta Receptor Interactions (PMC4751366)
- **Receptors documented:**
  - LRP1: Abeta efflux across BBB
  - RAGE: Abeta influx from plasma to brain
  - SCARA1/2: Microglial phagocytosis of fibrillar Abeta
  - PrPC: Oligomeric Abeta binding, mediates synaptic toxicity
  - NMDAR: Abeta oligomer interaction, excitotoxicity
- **Extractable reactions:**
  - `J_RAGE_influx: AB42_Plasma -> AB42_BrainISF; k_RAGE * RAGE_BBB * AB42_Plasma`
  - `J_LRP1_efflux: AB42_BrainISF -> AB42_Plasma; k_LRP1 * LRP1_BBB * AB42_BrainISF`
  - `J_PrPC_bind: AB42_oligomer + PrPC -> AB42_PrPC_complex; kon_PrPC * AB42_oligomer * PrPC`

### TREM2-Microglial Activation (PMC4605443, PMC4667728)
- **Mechanism:** TREM2-DAP12 signaling mediates microglial activation, phagocytosis
- **Key reactions:**
  - TREM2 ligand binding and DAP12 phosphorylation
  - Downstream Syk kinase activation
  - Microglial state transition (resting -> activated)
  - Phagocytosis of Abeta and apoptotic debris
- **Extractable as:**
  - `J_TREM2_activate: Microglia_resting -> Microglia_activated; k_TREM2 * TREM2_DAP12 * AB42_BrainISF`
  - `J_microglia_deactivate: Microglia_activated -> Microglia_resting; k_deact * Microglia_activated`
- **Rate type:** MA (mass action)

### Down Syndrome / Trisomy 21 APP Overproduction (PMC4678594)
- **Mechanism:** Extra copy of APP gene leads to ~1.5x APP expression
- **Extractable as:** Modified production rate parameter for APP
- **Note:** Provides validation case for APP production module
