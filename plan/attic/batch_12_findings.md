## 3. Extractable Reactions and Rate Laws

Based on pathway coverage and quantitative content from this batch:

### amyloid_processing

- **APP_cleavage_alpha**: `APP -> sAPPalpha + C83`
  - Rate law: MA or Michaelis-Menten | alpha-secretase (ADAM10/ADAM17)
- **APP_cleavage_beta**: `APP -> sAPPbeta + C99`
  - Rate law: Michaelis-Menten | BACE1 cleavage
- **C99_cleavage_gamma**: `C99 -> Abeta40/42 + AICD`
  - Rate law: Michaelis-Menten | gamma-secretase (PSEN1/2)

### abeta_dynamics

- **Abeta_production**: `-> Abeta_monomer`
  - Rate law: zero/first-order from C99 | Abeta generation
- **Abeta_aggregation**: `n*Abeta_mono -> Abeta_oligomer`
  - Rate law: nucleation-dependent polymerization | oligomerization
- **Abeta_fibrillization**: `Abeta_oligomer -> Abeta_fibril`
  - Rate law: first-order elongation | fibril formation
- **Abeta_degradation**: `Abeta -> degraded`
  - Rate law: first-order (NEP/IDE) | enzymatic clearance
- **Abeta_clearance_BBB**: `Abeta_brain -> Abeta_plasma`
  - Rate law: LRP1-mediated transport | BBB efflux

### tau_pathology

- **Tau_phosphorylation**: `Tau -> pTau`
  - Rate law: Michaelis-Menten (GSK3b/CDK5) | kinase-mediated
- **Tau_dephosphorylation**: `pTau -> Tau`
  - Rate law: Michaelis-Menten (PP2A) | phosphatase-mediated
- **pTau_aggregation**: `pTau -> NFT`
  - Rate law: nucleation-dependent | tangle formation

### neuroinflammation

- **Microglia_activation**: `Microglia_rest -> Microglia_act`
  - Rate law: signal-dependent (Abeta/DAMPs) | activation by Abeta
- **Cytokine_production**: `Microglia_act -> Cytokines`
  - Rate law: first-order | TNFa/IL1b/IL6 production
- **Abeta_phagocytosis**: `Abeta + Microglia -> cleared`
  - Rate law: Michaelis-Menten | microglial clearance
- **Complement_activation**: `C1q + Abeta -> complex`
  - Rate law: mass action | complement binding

### synaptic

- **Synapse_loss**: `Synapse -> lost`
  - Rate law: oligomer-dependent | toxicity
- **LTP_inhibition**: `LTP -> impaired`
  - Rate law: Abeta-dependent | plasticity impairment

### oxidative_stress

- **ROS_production**: `-> ROS`
  - Rate law: mitochondrial dysfunction | ETC impairment
- **ROS_clearance**: `ROS -> cleared`
  - Rate law: first-order (SOD/catalase/GSH) | antioxidant defense

### vascular_bbb

- **Abeta_efflux_LRP1**: `Abeta_brain -> Abeta_plasma`
  - Rate law: Michaelis-Menten (LRP1) | receptor-mediated transport
- **Abeta_influx_RAGE**: `Abeta_plasma -> Abeta_brain`
  - Rate law: Michaelis-Menten (RAGE) | receptor-mediated influx
- **BBB_degradation**: `BBB_intact -> BBB_impaired`
  - Rate law: inflammation-dependent | barrier breakdown

### protein_clearance

- **Autophagy**: `substrate -> autophagosome`
  - Rate law: mTOR-dependent | macroautophagy
- **Lysosomal_degradation**: `autophagosome -> degraded`
  - Rate law: first-order (pH-dependent) | lysosomal processing
